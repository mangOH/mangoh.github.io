5: Mounting Methods

5.1 Overview

This chapter describes methods and hardware specifications for mounting IoT Expansion Cards in Host applications.

Expansion cards are designed to be mounted in host applications via three methods to meet customer requirements:

•Plug in via rails. 3D-printing files are available at mangoh.io, and molded versions will be made available in future.

•Screwed into platform via spacers

•Screwed into platform via soldered standoffs
