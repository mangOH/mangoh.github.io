1.6 IoT Expansion Card Characteristics

Key characteristics of the IoT Expansion Card specification include:

•Standard footprint

•Support for wide expansion cards (up to 8 slots wide)

•Low-cost IoT Connectors

•Several available host interfaces:

·ADC

·GPIOs

·I2C

·I2S

·PCM

·PPS Clock (Stratum 1)

·SPI

·UART

·USB

·SDIO
