4.2 Mechanical Dimensions

4.2.1 Height Category

IoT Expansion Cards are assigned a height category based on the top height (distance from the top face of the PCB to the top of the tallest component on the PCB):

•Category 1: £ 14.00 mm

•Category 2: > 14.00 mm

4.2.2 Required Top / Bottom Clearance

Figure 4-1 illustrates the space requirements between the IoT Expansion Card and a host application.

Thus, maximum component heights on the expansion card’s top and bottom face are:

•Top:

·Category 1: £ 14.00 mm

·Category 2: > 14.00 mm

•Bottom — 2.5 mm (0.5 mm clearance required to host PCB)

 

Figure 4-1:   IoT Expansion Card Height Limits

Expansion cards may be designed to fit in 1–8 slots on a host device, depending on OEM design requirements (the expansion card slots are numbered beginning with slot 0). Figure 4-2 and Figure 4-3 describe the measurements for single-slot and double-slot IoT Expansion Cards respectively.
